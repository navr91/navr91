About

280x280

Welcome to Jana’s Dahlias, where dahlias rule.

What started three years ago as a backyard experiment quickly turned into a full-blown love affair with these wildly beautiful flowers. Now, my little patch of Santa Cruz soil is bursting with a rainbow of blooms each summer and fall. Each dahlia is grown with plenty of sunshine, care, and perhaps a few whispered compliments.

I sell fresh-cut dahlias at select local shops and pop ups during the season. And I offer tubers so you can start your own dahlia obsession right at home.

Want to stay in the loop or place an order? Follow me on Instagram @janasdahlias where I share the dahlia magic with you!
